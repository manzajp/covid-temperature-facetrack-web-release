About Us

In the current pandemic situation, all countries are fighting COVID-19 and are still looking for a practical and cost-effective solution to the problems that are arising in a variety of ways. Researchers in physical sciences and engineering are attempting to meet such challenges by developing new theories, describing new research problems, developing user-centered explanations, and educating ourselves and the public. The purpose of this brief review was to raise awareness of this innovative technology and its significant applications for the COVID-19 pandemic. That is when the IOT and real time processing comes handy to handle the situation. Real-time systems are now used in a wide range of applications, including avionics, automotive, and patient monitoring, to name a few. A Real-time System (RTS) is limited in its ability to carry out its functionality due to (strict) timing constraints. In this SDD the proposed system will provide a solution to detect probable COVID-19 patients around using IOT in real time.
